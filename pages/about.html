Our story

Swasthi LLP was founded in 1993 in Chennai, India. For over three decades, we've delivered IT services — from backend technical support and infrastructure management to digital transformation — building long-term client relationships based on reliability and technical depth.

Today, Swasthi is a Microsoft Partner, positioned at the intersection of enterprise IT and AI. Our services span AI adoption, Copilot deployment, data engineering, cloud modernisation, automation, and managed IT — with a growing focus on Industry 5.0 and predictive MRO.
